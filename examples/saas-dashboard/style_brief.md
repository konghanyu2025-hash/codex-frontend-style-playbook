# Style Brief

## Product Goal

Create a B2B revenue operations dashboard that helps sales leaders inspect pipeline health, forecast risk, and next actions in one pass.

## Target User

Sales leaders and revenue operations managers who revisit the dashboard daily. They need fast scanning, clear exceptions, and confidence in the data hierarchy.

## Required Screens or Sections

- Global navigation.
- Account or team filter row.
- Forecast summary.
- Pipeline health metrics.
- Risk queue.
- Deal movement timeline.
- Next-action panel.

## Information Density

High. The interface should prioritize scanning and comparison over editorial storytelling.

## References

- Modern CRM analytics dashboards.
- Financial operations tools with compact data tables.
- Observability consoles with clear alert severity.

Do not copy any reference brand, icon style, chart design, or written content.

## Visual Direction

Quiet, precise, and executive-readable. Use a restrained surface system, compact metrics, clear table hierarchy, and one strong accent for risk or recommended action.

## Constraints

- Must remain usable on a laptop viewport.
- Avoid marketing-page layout patterns.
- Avoid giant decorative cards.
- Charts should support comparison, not decoration.

## Anti Goals

- No oversized hero.
- No generic purple gradient dashboard.
- No fake glassmorphism.
- No unreadable micro charts.
